What data is stored?

To download the right packages, mangrove needs to transmit the following data to the source server:

  • The site UUID
    (a unique string of characters generated from your site address as an identifier)
  • PHP version
  • Database type and version
  • CMS name and version
    (if applicable)

Those details will be stored at the source server.

Additional choices (such as activating automatic updates) may require further data to be stored.

We care about your privacy and security and will always inform you about the data we transmit and store ahead of time.
