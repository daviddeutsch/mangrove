Hey there!

Just a heads up: To deliver the right packages to you, mangrove needs to tell our server a little bit about your setup.

This data is collected anonymously and is used so we can run statistics on it.

Of course, mangrove can function without a connection, so if you'd rather not share anything, or have decided against connecting to our server for some other reason, that's fine - it's just a little more work for you (you have to download and upload packages yourself).

We also offer you the option to send us a little more data which helps us make better decisions in the future (for instance on what software to integrate with). If you're willing to help us out there, you will get a month of access to valanx.org in return.

More about why we do this

There are two major reasons why we collect data:

First, the joomla landscape is quite diverse. Different versions of the main CMS, of components and of server software are in use. So we collect data get a tap on trends and figure out the state of technological development (like: "How many people have switched to Joomla 3.0 yet?", or "Is it safe to require PHP 5.3?").

Second, nothing beats knowing how people use applications and in which combination. That helps us, for instance, to make decisions on which components to integrate with next.
